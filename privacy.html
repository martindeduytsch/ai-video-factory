Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

AI Video Factory is a private creator automation tool used by the app owner to generate and publish short-form videos to their own social media account.

Information Collected

The app may access basic account information through third-party authentication only to confirm that the correct creator account is connected.

Authentication Tokens

After authorization, access and refresh tokens may be stored locally on the user's machine. These tokens are used only to maintain authentication and enable video upload or publishing features.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Local Storage

Generated videos, captions, logs, configuration files, and authentication tokens may be stored locally on the user's computer.
